WELCOME TO

Apostles Fellowship Outreach Churches INC

We are a Christ-centered ministry committed to proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, equipping believers, and transforming lives through the power of God's Word.

Our desire is to raise disciples who will impact nations according to the Great Commission.

Read More

Vision

Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost.

Matthew 28:19–20

Mission

Winning souls, discipling believers, raising leaders and establishing churches throughout the nations.
